# ParrotTranslator - Privacy Policy

[日本語のドキュメント](PRIVACY_POLICY.md)

---

## Introduction

ParrotTranslator is committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

---

## Information We Collect

The app stores the following information only on your device:

- App settings (language, volume, theme, etc.)
- Learning history (spoken content, translation results, evaluation results)

This information is not sent to external servers.

---

## Use of Information

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

- Providing app functionality
- Displaying learning history
- Persisting user settings

---

## Speech Recognition and Translation

This app uses Apple's Speech and Translation frameworks. When on-device processing is enabled, audio data is processed only on your device. If on-device processing is disabled, audio data may be sent to Apple's servers. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more details.

---

## Data Storage

All data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted externally. Deleting the app will remove all related data.

---

## Third-Party Services

This app uses the following Apple frameworks:

- **Speech Framework** (speech recognition)
- **Translation Framework** (translation)
- **AVFoundation** (audio playback)

For privacy information related to these services, please consult Apple's Privacy Policy.

---

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the app's App Store page.
